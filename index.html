About us

NurOS - A new GNU/Linux distribution that is not based on Arch, Debian, SUSE, or even Fedora/Red Hat Ent. and on pure Linux, just from scratch. It was created by the same users for the same users and has what users want, not companies and large organizations. Also, our distribution kit contains something that company distributions do not include. For example, from the GRUB theme box (including Beta-1.4, it has not yet been released) and the clipboard manager. Also coming from the box are: Neophyte, OpenRC, zsh and utilities that improve/replace the basic ones. And if we talk about ZSH, then of course there will be Oh My ZSH in the distribution.
